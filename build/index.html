About

MacroHacks is a 24-hour virtual hackathon managed and sponsored by MacroTech Solutions Inc. Hackers have a day to build any software/hardware project from the ground up with absolutely no restrictions and in the end, we will be awarding prizes to the winners. We will also have a wide range of workshops with skilled and experienced mentors to teach skills like Machine Learning, Python, Appwrite implementation in JavaScript, and many others. Hackers will have full access to mentors that can help them debug issues or develop their code to enable everyone to create awesome projects regardless of skill levels. We are dedicated to making MacroHacks a fun event for all hackers, so if you want the opportunity to learn new programming skills, build great projects, and compete for prizes, join us on June 27-28!


FAQ

If you have a question that isn't here, feel free to contact us.

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where a team of students can develop anything: a website, an app, or anything else. Let your creativity run wild!

Who runs MacroHacks?

MacroHacks is completely run by MacroTech Solutions Inc.

How much will it cost me?

Absolutely nothing! Just participate and have fun.

Do I need an idea or a team?

No! We will be running a team building event and there will be time for you to brainstorm an idea.

How do I apply?

Scroll to the top of this page and click the register button.
